🍿 SnackClub Terms of Use

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Overview of Service

SnackClub provides registered members with access to our curated inventory of treats and snacks. Membership tiers determine selection privileges and priority rights.

2. Membership Tiers & Rules

  • Basic Tier: Operates on a leftover model. Availability is not guaranteed.
  • Premium Tier: Distribution is cycle-based and randomized. Priority queue rules apply.
  • Platinum Tier: Members receive full control to pick items, restricted exclusively to our real-time active warehouse stock. Custom requests outside of listed stock are prohibited.

3. Financial Policy & Safeguards

To ensure steady purchasing cycles and secure premium products for the group, all received funds are directly deployed into stock acquisition instantly.

Strict No-Refund Safeguard: Because money turns into physical inventory immediately, all transactions and monthly plans are non-refundable and final.

4. Member Code of Conduct

Members must show respect for shared community inventory. Actively hoarding snacks, bypassing tier restrictions, or selling club assets to non-members will lead to an immediate ban without warning.
